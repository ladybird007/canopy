How do I get started with VisionPlus?

It's easy to get started, and even easier to use your benefits.

  1. 1. Create your VSP account.

    Review your vision benefits and access your eligibility and coverage information at vsp.com.

  2. 2. Find a VSP provider.

    Search thousands of VSP providers! Go to www.vsp.com/choicewithaffiliates or call 800-877-7195.

  3. 3. Visit eyeconic.com to find frames.

    Connect your VSP benefits, upload your prescription at checkout, and order your glasses or contacts online following your exam.
